# Options Extractor — Quick Guide

## What it does
You give it a company. It finds the annual report, pulls out the
stock-options / share-pay numbers, and gives you an **Excel file**.

## How to use it (3 ways)
1. **Search any company** *(easiest — use first)* → type company name and/or
   ticker → click **Extract options data**.
2. **A country tab** *(most accurate)* → click the country → type the ticker (an
   example is shown in the box) → click **Fetch & extract**.
3. **Upload a report** *(for any country with no tab, or Germany)* → drag in the
   PDF → done.

Then click **Download Excel**.

## Countries you can click
- **Americas:** United States, Canada, Brazil
- **Europe:** United Kingdom, Denmark, Germany (upload only),
  **All European Countries** (25 markets in one search)
- **Asia-Pacific:** Japan, South Korea, China, Hong Kong, India, Taiwan,
  Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Israel

Any other country → use **Search any company**, then **Upload a report** if
needed. Reports in any language come back in **English**.

## Speed (what takes longer)
- **Fast:** most countries.
- **A bit slower:** United Kingdom (scanned files take ~1 min).
- **Slower:** Indonesia, Israel (protected sites).
- **Big PDFs always take longer** to read, on any country.

## If it fails
- Add the **company name** (not just ticker), or pick the country.
- Still nothing? Download the report and use **Upload a report**.
- A **⚠** on a result row = check that number against the report.
